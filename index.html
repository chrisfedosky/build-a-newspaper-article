September 11, 2025

Leader of the Free World seen pantless!

Will we or won't we see little Willie?

F. Scott McFreedlove

In a scandalous moment of insurrection-level media daring, flamboyant photo-journalist Ezerald Collingsworth snapped the President of the United States enjoying his morning coffee pantless in the White House rose garden.

A repeating red lipstick "kiss" motif adorned the mostly white boxer briefs dangling below his untucked, pressed white dress shirt and tie loosely draped around his shoulders. When asked why his sock garters were ablaze with communist China's hammer-and-sickle logo, White House Press Secretary Bob Johnstone said: "No Comment."
